Islamic Life

Faith in action – prayer, community, and spiritual growth

Daily Worship

Prayer Accommodations

Five Daily Prayers

The school schedule includes breaks for Dhuhr and Asr prayers. A dedicated prayer hall is available. Students are encouraged to pray on time.

Jummah Salah

Every Friday, the entire school gathers for Jummah prayer. A student-led khutbah is delivered, fostering leadership and public speaking skills. Timings are adjusted accordingly.

Halal Food

The cafeteria serves 100% halal meals. No pork or non‑halal meat is allowed on campus. Students may also bring home‑cooked lunches.

Islamic Dress Code

All students must observe modest Islamic attire. Girls wear hijab and loose clothing; boys are required to cover from navel to knees. Uniforms are available.

Islamic Events

We celebrate and commemorate important Islamic occasions throughout the year.

Ramadan Iftar & Taraweeh nights
Eid-ul-Fitr & Eid-ul-Adha celebrations
Hajj simulation & Islamic heritage week
Quran competition & Seerah conference
Charity drives & community service days
