Academics

Modern education enriched by Islamic knowledge

Our Approach

Dual Curriculum Excellence

Modern STEAM

Our accredited program covers Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics with advanced placement options. Critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration are at the core.

Islamic Studies

Daily Quran, Arabic, and Islamic studies classes. Students memorize Quran, learn Fiqh, Seerah, and develop a deep love for their faith. All within an environment that respects Islamic rules.

Note: Islamic rules are observed — gender separation after a certain grade, modest dress code, halal meals, and prayer breaks are integrated into the schedule.

Grade Levels

Pre-K & Kindergarten

Play-based learning with Islamic manners and basic Arabic.

Elementary (1-5)

Strong foundation in core subjects, Quran memorization, and character building.

Middle School (6-8)

Advanced academics, leadership, and Islamic identity formation.

High School (9-12)

College prep, AP courses, and comprehensive Islamic worldview.
